### Linux 一键安装最新版 Golang                
从 `https://golang.org/dl/` 页面提取最新版本号，自动安装，设置环境变量。                
```sh
curl -LO https://raw.githubusercontent.com/koomox/devops/master/storage/linux/scripts/go/latest_go.sh
chmod +x ./latest_go_v2.sh
./latest_go.sh
```
